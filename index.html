Tallinn

Ranging from medieval castles and streets to modern districts with skyscrapers, Tallinn has to offer something for anyone. With such a wide variety of choices, Tallinn is a perfect destination for your next journey. Click here to learn more about things to do in Tallinn.


Info

Located on the shore of the Gulf of Finland of the Baltic Sea, Tallinn is the main financial and cultural centre of Estonia. Tallinn has a population of about 445,000 and administratively lies in the Harju maakond (county). Click here to learn more about the history of Tallinn.
